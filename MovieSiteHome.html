Visual affects

In action movies there is a lot of time spent creating the cgi and putting it onto the footage.

Jobs

When you see the end credits to movies, there are thousands of names because filmmaking takes lots of different roles.

Movies start with the producer who hires the director, pays for everything, and has executive control. Then there is the director who directs the filming and shots of the film. There is also the screenwriters that write the story and screenplay.

In front of the camera there are the leading actors, supporting actors and extras and day performers such as stuntmen or skilled performers.

There are also storyboard artists or previz artists, the casting director that chooses the actors, location managers, assistant directors that organise filming, camera crew, sound mixers, lighting department, set designers, costume hair and makeup departments, editors, sound designers, music supervisors, composers. Then there is the visual effects department with producers, directors and artists. Finally colorists.
