The Production Process

Filmaking is a long complicated, and expensive process. It is coposed of preproduction, filming, and post production.

This is the first item's accordion body. Before the filmimng, there is the pre production where the producer, director, and production manager plan out the film. There are storyboard artists or previz artists that map out the scenes. There is then the casting director that chooses the actors. There is also location managers who plan where to film.

This is the second item's accordion body. During the filming portion there is a whole team of assistant directors that organise filming. Then there is the camera crew that adjusts the lighting and angles and lenses. There are sound and lighting mixers, Set designers, and costume and hair and makeup departments.

This is the third item's accordion body. In Post production there is the editors who put together all the shots, the sound designers, music supervisors, and composers. Then there is the visual effects department with producers, directors and artists. Finally colorists corrects and colorgrades the fil.
